About Us

welcome to beautybar….

founded by Bella Charles, beautybar is expertly curated with the aim to educate and inspire.

we offer a coveted edit of products while focusing on delivering a truly personal and cheerful experience. our product selection is methodically researched and has a distinct emphasis on exceptional quality and lasting appeal. we work diligently to cut the crap; only products that meet or exceed our discerning standards will be considered. it’s simple: if we don’t love it, you will not find it at beautybar. you will find a welcoming and dynamic shopping experience where hands-on play is encouraged and personal service is paramount.

what started out in 2005 as a small brick and mortar boutique has grown to include a successful ecommerce store, exclusive brand collaborations and an expanded edit of curated luxuries.

as beautybar continues to grow the guiding principle will always be to offer extraordinary products in an upscale environment with exceptional customer service.

an unparalleled beauty experience offering you the finest products from around the globe.
